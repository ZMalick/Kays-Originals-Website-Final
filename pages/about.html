The Story Behind Kay's Originals

A passion for art. A space for artists. A gallery for everyone.

↗ View on Google Maps

Founded on a Love of Art

Kay Green started Kay's Originals with a simple belief: that original art should be accessible, personal, and celebrated. What began as a small studio space in Austin, Texas, has grown into a curated gallery representing visionary artists from around the world.

Every piece in the gallery is hand-selected by Kay and her team. We look for work that tells a story — art that is bold, authentic, and crafted with intention. Whether it's a sweeping oil painting, a delicate charcoal sketch, or a striking bronze sculpture, each piece is chosen because it moves us.

Our mission is to connect people with art that resonates. We believe that the right piece can transform a space, spark a conversation, and become part of your story.

What We Do

Services for Collectors & Artists

Kay's Originals is a small, independent gallery built around three core things we do well.

Curated Exhibitions

Rotating shows built around a theme, a medium, or a single artist — each one personally selected by Kay and her team.

Artist Consignment

We represent working artists and handle the gallery side — display, storytelling, and connecting their work to the right audience.

Community Programming

Opening nights, artist talks, and informal gallery visits that make original art feel approachable for everyone in Austin.
