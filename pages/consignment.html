Consignment at Kay's Originals

We partner with artists to share their work with a wider audience. Learn how to join our gallery.

How It Works

Our consignment process is simple and transparent.

1

Submit Your Work

Send us images of your artwork along with a brief artist statement. We welcome paintings, sculptures, and sketches in all media.

2

Gallery Review

Our curatorial team reviews every submission with care. We look for originality, craftsmanship, and a strong artistic voice.

3

Join the Gallery

Accepted artists become part of the Kay's Originals family. Your work is featured on our website and in our Austin gallery space.

What We Look For

Original Work

We represent only original artwork — no reproductions or prints. Each piece must be the artist's own creation.

Quality Craftsmanship

Attention to materials, technique, and finish matters. We look for work that demonstrates skill and care.

Artistic Vision

We value artists with a clear point of view. Your work should reflect a personal and consistent creative voice.

Professional Presentation

Artwork should be properly finished, documented, and ready for display. High-quality photographs are essential.
