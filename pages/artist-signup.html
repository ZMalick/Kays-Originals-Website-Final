Artist Application

Interested in showing your work at Kay's Originals? Download the application form and submit it by email.

How to Apply

  1. Download the application form using the button below.
  2. Fill out the form completely with your name, contact information, media, and artist statement.
  3. Attach approximately 5 sample JPEG images of your work. Please keep file sizes small (under 2 MB each).
  4. Email the completed form and images to applications@kaysoriginals.com.
Request Application Form

Email us and we'll send you the application form directly.

What Happens Next

1. Review

Our team reviews every application and looks for original work with a strong artistic voice. We typically ask for about 5 pieces initially.

2. Contact

If your work is a good fit, we'll reach out to schedule an appointment. We aim to respond within 2-3 weeks.

3. In-Person Visit

Bring your physical artwork to the gallery for an in-person review. We'll discuss consignment terms and select pieces to display (typically 3 from your initial submission).
